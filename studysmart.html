How to Study Smart?


Tips for Study Smart


  1. Sits in front of the class.
  2. Take notes by hand instead of using laptop.
  3. Simplify, summarize and compress the information.
  4. Do exercise at least 3 times a week.
  5. Drink at least 8 glasses of water a day.
  6. Reward ourselves at the end of each study session.
  7. Do not study until midnight, but study early in the morning.
  8. Sleep at least eight hours a night.
